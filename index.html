brief profile

A person having demonstrated history of working in technical education and holding a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering has a positive ideology for society development. He obtained preceding degrees i.e. B.Tech and M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Biju Patnaik University of Technology, Odisha. He has completed MBA with specialization Human Resource Management. Served in various positions in different premier institutes and organizations and guided students in UG and PG courses. Presently working at Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (DGT), India  through U.P.S.C, New Delhi and is actively engaged in conducting and supervising training programs and methodologies of different courses including IT, Networking  & Cloud Computing and others. He has authored international reputed publications in Journals, Conferences, Book, Book Chapters and others. During Ph.D. he worked along with his supervisor in the SERB(DST) funded Research Project on WASN. His areas of interest is to study Wireless Sensor Networks and related technologies, discover and design new active learning paradigm using technology to improvise teaching and training standards in the country. He is life member of Computer Society of India, member of Institute of Engineers (India) and internationally awarded "Best Research Reviewer" by College of Computer Science, University of Makati, Phillipines in 2017.
